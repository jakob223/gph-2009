Solution: High Expectations

The puzzle given to the solvers is a jumbled audio file with a number of musical tracks overlaid on top of one another. Broadway fans will doubtless recognize the songs of Lin-Manuel Miranda’s major smash hit “In the Heights”, and quickly identify each of the seven songs in the audio clip. Each audio clip can be identified as coming from a specific part of the song; here’s all the songs with the timestamps of the audio clip:
  • (2:23) It Won’t Be Long Now
  • (0:53) When You’re Home
  • (1:31) In the Heights
  • (0:41) Alabanza
  • (0:31) Inútil
  • (1:27) Hundreds of Stories
  • (0:43) Sunrise
As hinted by the flavor text, each of these timestamps is a “prime” number. If you imitate the way that the audio files were collapsed into one another, continuing to add the digits of each timestamp until you get a single digit, you get the following:
  • 7 It Won’t Be Long Now
  • 8 When You’re Home
  • 5 In the Heights
  • 5 Alabanza
  • 4 Inútil
  • 1 Hundreds of Stories
  • 7 Sunrise
Index each number into the song title, and you get BREATHE.
